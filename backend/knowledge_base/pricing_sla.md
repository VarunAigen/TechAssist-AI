# CloudNexus — Pricing & SLA

## Pricing Plans

### Free Tier
- **Price**: $0/month
- **Resources**: 1 project, 512 MB RAM, 1 vCPU
- **Bandwidth**: 10 GB/month
- **Support**: Community forum + email (48hr response)
- **Analytics**: 7-day retention
- **Best for**: Individual developers, proof of concept

### Professional Plan
- **Price**: $49/month per seat (billed annually) or $59/month (monthly billing)
- **Resources**: Unlimited projects, up to 16 GB RAM, 8 vCPUs per instance
- **Bandwidth**: 1 TB/month (additional at $0.10/GB)
- **Support**: Email (4hr response) + Live chat (business hours)
- **Analytics**: 90-day retention
- **Integrations**: All standard integrations included
- **SLA**: 99.9% uptime guarantee
- **Best for**: Growing teams, small to mid-size companies

### Enterprise Plan
- **Price**: Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **Resources**: Unlimited — dedicated infrastructure available
- **Bandwidth**: Unlimited
- **Support**: 24/7 priority support + dedicated account manager + quarterly business reviews
- **Analytics**: 365-day retention + custom dashboards
- **Integrations**: All integrations + custom integration support
- **SLA**: 99.99% uptime guarantee
- **Security**: SSO, CMEK, VPC peering, data residency controls
- **Best for**: Large enterprises, regulated industries

## Volume Discounts
- 10-49 seats: 10% discount
- 50-199 seats: 15% discount
- 200+ seats: 20% discount + dedicated support pod

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

### Uptime Commitments
| Plan | Monthly Uptime % | Max Downtime/Month |
|------|------------------|--------------------|
| Free | Best effort | No guarantee |
| Professional | 99.9% | 43.8 minutes |
| Enterprise | 99.99% | 4.3 minutes |

### SLA Credits
If CloudNexus fails to meet the uptime commitment:
- 99.0% - 99.9%: 10% credit of monthly bill
- 95.0% - 99.0%: 25% credit of monthly bill
- Below 95.0%: 50% credit of monthly bill

Credits are applied automatically to the next billing cycle. Customers do not need to file a claim.

### Exclusions
SLA does not cover:
- Scheduled maintenance (announced 72 hours in advance)
- Force majeure events
- Customer-caused issues (misconfiguration, exceeding limits)
- Beta/preview features

## Billing

### Payment Methods
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfer / ACH (Enterprise plans)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise plans, net-30 terms)

### Invoicing
- Monthly plans: Charged on the 1st of each month
- Annual plans: Charged on the anniversary of signup
- Invoices available in the dashboard under Billing → Invoices
